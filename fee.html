Payment Procedure

If you want to participate in the conference, there are four steps that you need to follow to make a payment in the ICST 2023.

Presenter

Fee International Participant Domestic Participant
Early bird discount (until September 25) US$275 Rp275
Regular US$275 Rp275
Additional paper(s) US$275 Rp275
Publication (per paper)* US$275 Rp275

Non-Presenter

Fee International Participant Domestic Participant
Early bird discount (until September 25) US$275 Rp275
Regular US$275 Rp275

Payments cannot be made in cash; they must be via credit card or bank transfer to the BioMIC UGM account below:

Payment via credit card (USD)

For international presenters who submit their manuscript to the EDAS system. We do not recommend local presenters to pay the conference fee via credit card because the EDAS payment currency is USD.

Payment via bank account (USD)

The payment account will be shown on the day after the last notification of acceptance.

Payment via bank account (IDR)

The payment account will be shown on the day after the last notification of acceptance.

Before confirming your payment in the confirmation form, you need to prepare these following items:

  • The scanned or screenshot of your payment proof in JPG / JPEG / PDF format.
  • Your paper ID(s).
  • The presenter name.

  • Wait for the next 2-3 business days for us to verify your payment.
  • Make sure not to exceed the specified deadline.
  • The payment confirmation deadline is on October 2, but you will get an early-bird discount if you pay before September 25.
  • If you did not pay via credit card in the EDAS, you do not need to click the register icon in the EDAS. The committee will register it for you after reviewing your payment.
  • If you still require further assistance, please feel free to contact us.
