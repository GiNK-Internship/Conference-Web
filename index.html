About

The International Indonesia Conference on Interdisciplinary Studies (IICIS) is an international annual interdisciplinary studies organized by the Faculty of Social and Political Sciences (FISIP), University of Lampung, Indonesia. In the first conference, several distinguished speakers from five different countries, namely, the United States of America, Australia, Croatia, Indonesia, and Malaysia. Following up on the first conference, this 2021 conference will continue to discuss more current relevant issues on diverse themes.
